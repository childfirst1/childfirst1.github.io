Learn, Share, Play

Experience the difference in quality at Childfirst, where children are always safe and having a fantastic time with friends!

New friendships are cultivated, independence is encouraged, and essential communication skills are fostered through the principles of kindness and respect.

Did You Know?

Since our program is housed inside Mitchell Elementary, children have a safe walk to and from their classrooms. Children in Kindergarten are escorted by staff until they reach Grade 1.
