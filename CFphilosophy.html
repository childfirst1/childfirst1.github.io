Childfirst Childcare's Guiding Principles:

  • Always maintain a child's dignity and sense of self.
  • Value each child's family and cultural influences.
  • Teach peaceful words, gentle actions, and acceptance.
  • Listen carefully to a child's thoughts and perspective.
  • Empower each child to navigate their own path.

Our programs are designed to challenge curiousity, encourage self-expression, and honour diversity.
