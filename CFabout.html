About Us

Childfirst Childcare has been operating since 2006 in Richmond, B.C. Over the past decade, we have created wonderful memories and built many valuable relationships with the children in our care and their families.

We look forward to seeing you!

Our Teachers

Childfirst's teachers possess an array of skills that enrich the learning experiences of our children. All have a post-secondary education grounded in child development and experience working with school age children.

We fulfill all requirements of BC's Child Care Licensing Regulation which includes a clear criminal record check and first aid certification.

Our Centre

Childfirst is designed to ensure that children have open access to art supplies, books, toys and other program equipment. Little imaginations flourish as children go about the important business of preparing for the day's activities.

Our centre is an inviting and comfortable second home to the children in our care!
