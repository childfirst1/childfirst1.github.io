Childfirst Childcare Team

We are a team of dedicated teachers from diverse backgrounds. We believe in gentle guidance and taking the time to know each and every child in our care.

Teresa

Teresa is the director of Childfirst Childcare. She has dedicated her knowledge and experience in designing the best quality childcare possible. Childfirst's Guiding Principles are based on her vision and her consistent priority to maintain a safe, inclusive, and fun environment for all children to grow and flourish.




Rebecca

Rebecca is the current manager at Childfirst Childcare. She is a kind and compassionate educator and a positive team leader. She is dedicated to running a high quality daycare, and is always brainstorming new activities and curriculums based on the children's interests. She welcomes any questions or concerns you may have and looks forward to meeting all families.



Amy

Amy is a patient and playful teacher who encourages active learning, building strong self-esteem, and exploring creativity. She believes in finding the positives in any situation, and this is reflected in her approach to guiding children. She can often be found teaching at the arts & crafts table, or listening to everyone's stories during snack time.



Chelsey

Chelsey is a gentle teacher who is bilingual - fluent in both English and French! The children benefit greatly from her French language skills as she is often assisting with French homework and reading. A friendly teacher who is always ready to listen and help.
