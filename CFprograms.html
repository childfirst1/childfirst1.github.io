Our Program

Childfirst Childcare provides outstanding child care throughout the entire year!

Before and After School Care (7:00am - 6:00pm)

  • Inclusive programs for children in Kindergarten to 12 years old.
  • Full time, Part time, and Drop-in care available as space permits.
  • Open on Pro-D days and extended hours for Early Dismissals.
  • A healthy afternoon snack is provided (an additional morning snack provided on Pro-D days).
  • We accept Childcare Subsidy and are happy to assist with the application process.

School Breaks

We are open for Winter Break, Spring Break and Summer Vacation from 7:00 am to 6:00 pm.

  • Field trips to local attractions.
  • Exciting centre activities.
  • New friendships and adventures.
  • Fees include a healthy morning and afternoon snack each day.
  • Fees include all admission and transportation costs.

Did you know?

Children may attend our summer program the year that they begin Kindergarten.

This is a great way for Kinders to meet schoolmates and transition to their very important first year of school.

At Childfirst, older children often take initiative to act as kind leaders and help younger children who are new to the program!

Snacks

Childfirst's snack menu was developed with the guidance of a registered dietician. Our food choices are healthy and make up part of a child's daily nutritional requirements.

At Childfirst, snack time is a social event! Snack is available between 2:30 and 4:30 and children choose when and with whom they would like to eat.

They are encouraged to use good manners and to serve and clean up after themselves.
